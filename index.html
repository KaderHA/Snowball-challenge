The Family App

About us

Donating money without knowing or seeing what it actually does for people can sometimes be hard. If only there was a way for us to see the effect our donations have on people in need.

We are a newly developed company that are collaborating with Save the Children to work towards the goal of ending world hunger.

The app

Introducing The Family app! This app makes it possible for you to donate from wherever you are to people in developing countries that need your help. Through this app you get assigned a family in a country of your choice. In return you get to see what the money gets spend on and get to see the difference your money makes.

Through these difficult times you won’t be able to meet the family in person, but get to talk to them at least once a month. When this horrible pandemic is over it will of course be possible to meet the family yourself. There is also a function that makes it possible for multiple people to donate to one family as a group of 5 or less. The daily donation should be at least 10 kr but can’t be over 200 kr. This way you get to do a good deed every single day! You can ofc course quit donating whenever you like, all you have to do is end the subscription.
